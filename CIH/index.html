Who We Are

Kenya's Gateway to Global Hospitality Careers

Cascade Institute of Hospitality (CIH) is a leading hotel school located in the heart of Thika Town, Kiambu County. Founded on principles of quality, affordability, and unwavering standards, CIH has spent over a decade transforming young Kenyans into world-class hospitality professionals.

As one of only 12 CTH Gold Centres worldwide, we hold ourselves to international standards in everything we do — from our kitchen facilities to our tourism management classrooms. Our mission is simple: provide quality, holistic education and training that creates economically independent, globally competitive graduates.

Admissions & How to Apply

Admission Process

Simple Steps to
Join Our Community

1
Choose Your Programme
Browse our 56+ programmes across Culinary Arts, Hospitality, Tourism, ICT, and Foreign Languages. Find the one that matches your passion and career goals.
2
Submit Your Application
Fill in the enquiry form, email admissions@cascade.ac.ke, or walk into any of our 3 campuses. Bring your national ID and academic certificates.
3
Receive Admission Letter
Our admissions team reviews your application within 3 working days and sends a formal offer letter with your reporting date and fee structure.
4
Pay & Report
Pay the required fees and report on your intake date. We'll handle everything else — including your free foreign language class and industrial attachment placement.
Requirements
KCSE Certificate (D+ and above) · National ID / Passport · 2 Passport Photos · Application Fee
