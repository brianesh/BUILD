Welcome to Thika High School — an institution of immense historical significance and enduring excellence. Founded in 1951 and moved to our current home in 1956, we have spent the last 70 years shaping the minds and characters of Kenya's finest sons.

At Thika High School, we take pride in being more than just an institution of learning — we are a family dedicated to shaping the minds and character of our students. Our commitment is to provide a holistic education that nurtures discipline, academic excellence, and integrity while empowering our students to face the future with confidence.

We believe that education is a partnership between the school, parents, and the wider community. With the dedication of our teachers, the support of parents, and the enthusiasm of our students, we continue to build a strong foundation for success. Our focus goes beyond academics, as we encourage participation in sports, clubs, leadership opportunities, and community service to ensure that every child develops their unique potential.

As we celebrate our 70th Platinum Anniversary, we look ahead with great optimism. With the government's commitment to new infrastructure — 35 classrooms, a 1,000-bed dormitory, and a 2,000-seat multipurpose hall — Thika High School is poised for an even greater future. I warmly welcome you to be part of our journey.

JM
Mr. Julius Muraya Chief Principal, Thika High School | Platinum Jubilee 2026

Admissions 2026

How to Join Thika High School

1

Ministry of Education Selection

Form 1 admissions are conducted by the Ministry of Education Kenya based on KCPE results. Thika High is an extra-county school requiring strong performance.

2

Download Your Admission Letter

Selected students download their admission letter from the Ministry of Education official portal and report with all required items.

3

Grade 10 (CBE) — Apply Directly

For the new Grade 10 CBE pathway, contact the school directly. Pathways include STEM, Social Sciences, and Arts & Sports Science.

4

Contact School for Enquiries

For any admissions enquiries, contact the school on +254 723 705 899 or email thika.high@yahoo.com. The administration team is happy to assist.
